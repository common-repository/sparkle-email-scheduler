== Description ==
Sparkle Email Scheduler is a Free WordPress plugin to allow your potential customers to receive an email. You can schedule an email to your potential customers based on order status. For pending users, you can send an offer or discounts codes to them to attract them to complete their payments. 

= Features: =
* Easy to configure and schedule an email based on order status.
* Email schedule for every 5 mins, every 6 hours, every 12 hours, daily, every 2 days, every 3 days, weekly.
* Abandoned Cart Email 
* Customize your own email subject and email body as per your need.
* Selection options available based on payment or order status.  
* Supports for both Easy Digital Downloads and WooCommerce.
* Create as many email templates as you want.
* <strong>Support</strong> - Dedicated email, forum support.
* <strong>Free updates</strong> - Get free updates for lifetime.

== Installation ==
1. Unzip sparkle-email-scheduler.zip
2. Upload all the files to the /wp-content/plugins/sparkle-email-scheduler
3. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress.
4. For customizing the plugin\'s settings, click on the Sparkle Email Scheduler menu in WordPress left admin menu.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What does this plugin do? =
This plugin provides the ability to allow admin users to send/schedule emails based on order status for Easy Digital Downloads and WooCommerce.

= Do I need to configure email body? =
Yes , you will need to configure email body. You can use your required email body to be sent to users as per your need. You can use it as promotional mail to your potential customers.
